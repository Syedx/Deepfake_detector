About Our Project

Meet the team behind the deepfake detection technology

Our Mission

The Deepfake Detector project was born out of a need to combat the rapidly growing threat of manipulated media in our digital world. As deepfake technology becomes more advanced and accessible, the potential for misinformation and fraud increases.

Our mission is to develop and provide accessible, accurate, and transparent tools that help individuals and organizations verify the authenticity of digital media. We believe that by making detection technology widely available, we can help maintain trust in digital content.

The project combines cutting-edge computer vision techniques, deep learning models, and metadata analysis to create a comprehensive detection system that analyzes visual content from multiple perspectives.
